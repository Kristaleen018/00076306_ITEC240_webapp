About Us

The Ministry of Tourism, Culture and the Arts of Trinidad and Tobago is committed to making the country a premier tourist destination, with due consideration of the country’s abundant cultural and artistic heritage. By implementing strategic policy and research, the Ministry is seeking to develop sustainable tourism and cultural sectors which will provide a meaningful contribution to the socio-economic development of Trinidad and Tobago, provoke a feeling of national identity and pride in the nation’s citizens. The Ministry goes ahead to ensure that there is an enabling environment for these sectors to flourish while allowing locals and visitors an opportunity to enjoy the unique beauty and vibrancy of the Islands.
